# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Clients, brands, or partners who want to book affiliate marketing services. Admin users who manage the appointments.

## Product Purpose
To serve as the public face of Affiliate Live Co., providing service information and allowing users to seamlessly book appointments without logging in.

## Brand Personality
Professional, dynamic, trustworthy, modern agency.

## Anti-references
Generic templates, outdated corporate sites, purely text-heavy documentation portals.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell. 
- Expert confidence.
- Clear and effortless booking flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure high contrast, accessible forms for booking, and clear responsive design across mobile and desktop.
